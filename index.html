How It Works

Our innovative platform connects ambitious entrepreneurs with smart investors, creating a win-win ecosystem for business growth and wealth creation.

1

Entrepreneur Investment

Business owners invest 51% of the required capital, maintaining majority control while sharing the opportunity with investors.

2

Investor Participation

Multiple investors contribute the remaining 49% with a minimum 5% investment, becoming official shareholders in the business venture.

3

Profit Distribution

After a 6-month lock-in period, monthly profits are distributed to all shareholders based on their investment percentage.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Entrepreneurs

What types of businesses can apply for funding?

+

We will fund businesses across various sectors including hospitality, retail, automotive services, manufacturing, and professional services. The key criteria are proven market demand, scalability potential, and strong business fundamentals. Our platform is designed to support ventures with growth potential.

How much funding can I get for my business?

+

Funding amounts will vary based on your business needs and investor interest. We're planning to fund businesses from ₹5 lakhs to ₹5 crores. You need to invest 51% of the total requirement yourself to maintain majority control.

What ongoing support do you provide?

+

Beyond funding, we will provide comprehensive business mentorship, marketing support through our "Powered by Tanz Corp" badge program, networking opportunities, and access to our growing business ecosystem for cross-promotions and collaboration.

For Investors

What is the minimum investment amount?

+

The minimum investment is 5% of the total business funding requirement. This typically ranges from ₹25,000 to ₹10 lakhs depending on the business size and requirements.

How are returns calculated and distributed?

+

Returns are distributed monthly based on actual business profits and your investment percentage. After the 6-month lock-in period, you receive your share of profits every month along with detailed financial reports.

What legal protections do investors have?

+

All investments are protected through legal shareholder agreements, regular financial audits, and transparent reporting. You become an official shareholder with legal rights and protections under Indian corporate law.
